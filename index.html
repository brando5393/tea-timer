Simple Tea Timer

Lets face it, for a tea lover nothing beats a good cup of tea. The hardest part about making a cup of tea however, is waiting for it to steep, and then cool down enough to be drinkable. While this app can't tell you the appropriate temperature of your tea, it can at least help you through the first part of the process.

To use this app:

  1. First, select the brand of tea being brewed
  2. Next, select the variety of tea
  3. Then select weather to use the recommended steeping duration or set a custom one
    4. Please note that the brand of "other" automatically requires a custom steeping duration to be set.
  4. Click the start steeping button and enjoy some fun facts and peaceful music while you wait for your tea. ðŸ˜„
